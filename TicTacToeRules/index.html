    Tic-Tac-Toe Rules

    These are the rules of the game

    This game can be played with 1 or 2 players.

    Players take turns marking any empty space on the 3x3 grid. Player 1 places X's and player 2 places O's.

    When someone makes a 3 space long line horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, they win. If the board is filled and nobody wins, it is a tie.

    In 1 player mode, you play as Player 1 against the computer. The computer wins or ties every game.

    At the bottom of the page, it shows who's turn it is and what moves were played.

    Restart the game by reloading the page.

    The game mode button switches between 1 and 2 player mode. The autoplay button constantly plays random moves against the computer.
