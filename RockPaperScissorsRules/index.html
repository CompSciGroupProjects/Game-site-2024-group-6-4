    • Welcome To Rock Paper Scissors!

    These are the rules to the game

    This game is very simple. All you have to do is press 1 of 3 buttons

    When entering the game, you will be presented with 3 buttons: Rock, Paper, and Scissors.

    To play the game, you have to click one of these buttons and a computer will automatically select a choice

    After you and your computer choose an option, your results will print below in 3 categories.

    These categories are: Player Choice, Computer Choice, and Result.

    The player choice displays what button you clicked above.

    The computer choice displays the randomly selected option that the computer chose.

    The result shows if you won, the computer won, or if the result is a tie.

    You can play as many times as you like. Have fun Playing!
